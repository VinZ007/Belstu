Season 2023

The 2023 Formula 1 season was defined by Max Verstappen's unprecedented dominance, who won 19 out of 22 races and secured his third consecutive World Drivers' Championship title for Red Bull Racing, which also won the Constructors' Championship.

Notable elements included the addition of the Las Vegas Grand Prix, a new event on the calendar, and significant strides in Formula 1's sustainability efforts, such as the introduction of the first Impact Report and the use of biofuel-powered trucks for European logistics.

Season 2024

The 2024 Formula 1 season was a year of unexpected drama, featuring a record seven different winners, a thrilling constructor's championship battle that went down to the final race, and impressive performances from Lando Norris and McLaren, who emerged as the biggest challengers to Max Verstappen's dominance.

Verstappen ultimately secured his fourth consecutive Drivers' Championship, though less decisively than in previous years, while sustainability efforts focused on reducing the sport's carbon footprint through increased use of Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) and optimizing the race calendar.
